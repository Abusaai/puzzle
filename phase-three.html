Phase Three – The Final Step

Winds guide the longships through fates untold.
honor binds the shield wall with silent resolve.
all warriors bleed before they rise.
the sword remembers no mercy.
he who fears death has died already.
a promise broken can never be reforged.
storms do not ask permission to come.
to feast, one must first hunt in the forest.
only the brave taste the gold and mead of glory.
blood answers blood.
even kings kneel before steel.
brotherhood is forged, not given.
rivers remember fallen oars.
one shield is no army.
kin is tested when swords are drawn.
every axe tells a story.
northern winds never lie.
before the end, stand tall.
every path leads home.
fury grants no second chance.
only the gods know who falls.
rage fades, but names remain.
even shadows fear the sun.
your blade speaks when words fail.
only fools forget oaths.
unseen wounds cut deepest.
strength is not silence, but control.
echoes never lie.
ice breaks even the strongest shield.
time remembers.
